🏕️ Windmere Hollow (A2 – Main Hub)

An enchanted forest glade lit by bioluminescent trees. All paths seem to lead here. The air is silent, and flames never catch.

  • NPC: Old Syllan, exiled druid
  • Creature: Hollow Fox, trickster guide
  • Lore: "Windmere listens. It does not forget."

🎯 Quest Hooks

  • Echoes Beneath: Clear rubble from Echo Mine and extract a memory-stone.
  • The Trickster’s Trust: Win three riddles from the Hollow Fox to find a secret path.
  • Barrier of the Past: Reclaim the Southern fort and uncover its last stand.
  • Statue Garden: Solve the Stone Husk statues' lines to reveal a buried artifact.
  • The Ice Mirror: Retrieve the mirrored sword from Peak of Ice.
  • Cleansing the Cradle: Resolve growing tension in Cradle Village before it explodes.
